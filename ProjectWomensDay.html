Women’s Day
2024 Public.gr

Campaign identity for Public.gr’s Women’s Day 2024. I created the visuals, offer templates, landing page, and newsletters, using bold gradients, orange accents, and B&W portraits to celebrate every confident ‘NAI’ and ensure a consistent brand presence.

Programs

design
Process

Colours

Vibrant purple/pink gradients with orange highlights convey celebration and momentum, balanced by clean white for legibility.

Typography

A strong, rounded display type emphasizes the “NAI” message, supported by a modern sans-serif for product and promo details.

Aesthetics

Dynamic, cut-out figures and flowing wave motifs create movement and optimism, blending editorial energy with Public’s brand style for a fresh, empowering feel.

General Overview

Built for flexibility across web, mobile, social, and email, the system kept offers readable, visuals punchy, and the message unified—driving engagement throughout the Women’s Day period.
