Introduction
Hello! I'm Foteini, a senior creative designer with a passion for bringing ideas to life through compelling visuals. I thrive on blending creativity with strategy to deliver designs that are both beautiful and effective. Since 2016, I've had the pleasure of working as a designer, honing my skills and evolving with the ever-changing landscape of visual communication.
Experience
My professional journey includes experience in print design, which laid the foundation for my seamless shift into the dynamic world of web. I am currently a senior digital designer at public.gr, where I create engaging and intuitive user experiences that are focused on user-centric design, and are stunning and effective.
